⚙️ Setup Guide

How to use this calendar on any device — phone, tablet, or computer — so it's always with you.

1
Save this file somewhere permanent

Download this HTML file and save it to Google Drive, Dropbox, or iCloud Drive. That way it's backed up and accessible from anywhere.

2
Open it in your browser

On your phone: open the Google Drive / Dropbox app → tap the file → tap "Open in browser." On your computer: just double-click the file to open it in Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

3
Add to your phone's home screen (iPhone)

Open the file in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → tap Add to Home Screen → tap Add. It will appear like an app icon.

4
Add to home screen (Android)

Open in Chrome → tap the 3-dot menu → tap Add to Home Screen. Done — opens like an app.

5
Sync your Airbnb & VRBO bookings

Tap the 📥 iCal button, paste your export links from each platform, tap Import. Repeat anytime you want fresh data. Your manual entries are never overwritten.

6
Keep a master copy in Google Drive

Since entries are stored in the file itself while you use it, download/save an updated copy to Google Drive periodically so you never lose data if you switch devices.

No account needed. This calendar works entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. Your data stays with you.
